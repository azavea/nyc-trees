Collecting data on street trees leads to a greener and greater NYC!

Our street trees make our city cooler, our air cleaner, and our neighborhoods more beautiful. TreesCount! 2015 is New York City’s third decadal effort to inventory our urban forest, and we need tree-loving citizens like you to help us collect data. Volunteers will be trained on how to identify and assess street trees, using simple site surveying tools and software to map them block by block. For more information on the volunteer experience check out our FAQ!

Can we count on you? Create an account to become a voluntreer today!
